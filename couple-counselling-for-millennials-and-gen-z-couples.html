FAQs: Couple Counselling for Millennials and Gen Z Couples

Is couple counselling effective for young couples?

Yes. Studies show that structured relationship therapy significantly improves communication and satisfaction.

How long does couple counselling take?

Yes. Online therapy is proven to be as effective as in-person sessions when guided by licensed professionals.
