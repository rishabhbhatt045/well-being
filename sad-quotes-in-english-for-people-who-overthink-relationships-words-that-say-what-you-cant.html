  2. Uncertainty triggers anxiety

  4. Reassurance temporarily calms the mind

FAQs

Why do I overthink my partner’s behavior?

Your brain seeks emotional certainty. Lack of clarity triggers mental prediction.

Can overthinking damage relationships?

Yes. It creates reactions based on imagination rather than reality.

Do sad quotes actually help emotionally?

They validate feelings, reducing emotional pressure and loneliness.

When should I seek counselling?

If reassurance never feels enough and thoughts affect mood daily.

Is online counselling effective for overthinking?

Structured communication reduces assumptions significantly.
