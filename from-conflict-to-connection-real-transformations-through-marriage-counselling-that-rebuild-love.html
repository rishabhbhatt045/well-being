What Is Marriage Counselling Really Like?

Many people imagine therapy as awkward or blame-focused. In reality, it’s supportive and practical.

Marriage counselling is a guided conversation led by a trained expert who helps both partners:

  • Express feelings safely
  • Understand each other’s perspective
  • Break unhealthy patterns
  • Learn better communication tools
  • Rebuild trust and connection

At BetterLyf, counselling is solution-focused and balanced.
