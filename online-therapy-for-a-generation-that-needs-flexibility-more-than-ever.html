What Is Online Therapy?

Online therapy is professional mental health support delivered through digital platforms using video calls, audio sessions, or chat-based communication. It offers the same evidence-based care as traditional therapy—without travel or fixed locations.

Why Online Counselling Feels Less Intimidating

Many people delay therapy because they fear judgment or don’t know how to start. Online counselling reduces pressure by offering privacy, familiarity, and emotional safety—especially for first-time users.

Is Online Therapy Effective?

Research shows that online therapy is just as effective as in-person therapy for anxiety, depression, and stress when delivered by qualified professionals.
