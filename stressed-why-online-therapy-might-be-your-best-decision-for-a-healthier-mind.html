What Is Online Therapy?

Online therapy (also called online counselling or virtual therapy) is professional psychological support delivered through:

  • Video sessions
  • Audio calls
  • Chat-based therapy
  • Secure messaging

Instead of visiting a clinic, you connect with a licensed psychologist from your home.

At BetterLyf, online therapy sessions are conducted through secure, confidential platforms, ensuring privacy and comfort.

Does Online Therapy Actually Work?

Yes — research supports it.

Studies show online cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) is as effective as in-person therapy for many conditions like anxiety and depression.

The key? Consistency and professional guidance.

FAQ: Online Therapy

1. Is online therapy safe and confidential?

Yes. Reputable platforms like BetterLyf use secure systems and maintain strict confidentiality.

2. How many sessions will I need?

It depends on your concerns. Some people benefit from 6–8 sessions, while others prefer ongoing support.

3. Is online therapy suitable for severe depression?

It can help, but severe cases may require combined treatment (therapy + psychiatric consultation).

4. Can I switch therapists if I’m not comfortable?

Yes. Finding the right therapist is important for progress.
